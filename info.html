Info

My time is spent within graphic design, visual media and design education. My work is interdisciplinary, fusing image, typography, visual programming, moving image, sound, and physical computing. I predominantly work on independent projects, with practitioners within the arts sector on projects that have creative enquiry at their centre. I make enquiries as to how graphic design, conceptual approach and technology can blend into new meaningful socially engaged communications.

Within design education I contribute to a broad, multi-disciplinary and critically informed learning environment where students explore and apply graphic design in a range of innovative, creative and reflective ways.

As a freelance designer and artworker, I have worked in a wide range of London graphic design studios that included BBC Worldwide, Rufus Leonard, BHOFCB, and worked on accounts for Nokia, HP, Waitrose, Castrol, Iberia.

I am a graduate of Goldsmiths College, and the Royal College of Art (MA).
I hold a PGCHE in Higher Education and am a fellow of the HEA.
